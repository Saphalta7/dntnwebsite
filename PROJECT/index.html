Barley and Board Cafe in Denton, Texas, is a charming and unique dining establishment known for its welcoming atmosphere and innovative culinary offerings. At Barley and Board, guests can savor a diverse menu that features farm-to-table ingredients, craft beers, and an impressive selection of wines. The cafe is celebrated for its creative use of barley, evident in dishes like barley risotto and hearty barley-based salads. South Lakes Park is accessible to all ages with amenities including picnic tables, grills, basketball courts, soccer goals, dog stations, trails and more. The park is a great location for any nature lover, pet owner, or outdoor enthusiast. Take a walk with your furry friends in the trails and enjoy the view of the lake or go fishing. Feeling a bit competitive, take advantage of the sand volleyball courts, no reservation needed. Regardless of what you decide, enjoy the park with friends and family and unwind.
